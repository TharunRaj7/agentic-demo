CAMPUS GUIDE — UNIVERSITI LAKEWOOD MALAYSIA
===========================================

GENERAL INFORMATION
-------------------
Universiti Lakewood Malaysia (ULM) is a mid-sized research university located in Petaling Jaya, Selangor.
Founded in 1923, the university serves approximately 18,000 undergraduate and 5,000 graduate students across six faculties.

Campus Address: 1 Jalan Universiti, Petaling Jaya, Selangor 47301
Main Switchboard: +60 3-5550-0100
Website: www.ulm.edu.my

BUILDINGS & LOCATIONS
---------------------
- Perpustakaan Hartwell: Main library, study rooms, printing services (Building A, north quad)
- Ridgeline Science Complex: Biology, Chemistry, Physics departments (Building B)
- Dewan Pengasas: Administrative offices, Registrar, Financial Aid (Building C)
- The Commons: Main dining hall, cafe, food court (Building D, central campus)
- Eastbrook Hall: Computer Science and Engineering (Building E)
- Morton Arts Centre: Art, Music, Theatre departments and performance venues (Building F)
- Alpine Student Centre: Student clubs, lounges, bowling alley, game room (Building G)
- Summit Recreation Centre: Gym, pool, climbing wall, fitness classes (Building H)
- Pinecrest Residences: On-campus housing (Buildings R1–R6, west side of campus)
- Health & Wellness Centre: Student health services, counseling (Building J)
- Lakewood Innovation Hub: Startup incubator, maker space, co-working (Building K)

DINING OPTIONS
--------------
- The Commons Dining Hall: Buffet-style dining with Malaysian and international dishes.
  Sample menu: Nasi lemak, roti canai, char kuey teow, chicken rice, mee goreng, local desserts (cendol, kuih).
  Hours: Mon–Fri 7am–9pm, Sat–Sun 9am–8pm
  Accepts: Meal plans, Dining Credits, cash/card

- Summit Café (Recreation Centre): Coffee, smoothies, grab-and-go snacks, local drinks like teh tarik and Milo ais.
  Hours: Mon–Fri 6:30am–8pm, Sat–Sun 8am–6pm

- The Perch (Student Centre, 3rd floor): Full-service restaurant with campus view.
  Menu: Fusion of Malaysian and Western cuisine, nasi goreng, laksa, sandwiches, pasta.
  Hours: Mon–Fri 11am–9pm, Sat–Sun noon–8pm

- Quick Bites Food Court (Student Centre, 1st floor): Local hawker-style stalls.
  Sample options: Satay, rojak, wantan mee, burgers, sushi, fried rice, spring rolls.
  Hours: Mon–Fri 10am–10pm, Sat–Sun 11am–9pm

- Hartwell Brew Bar (Library): Specialty coffee, pastries, kuih-muih, and light Malaysian snacks.
  Hours: Mon–Thu 7am–10pm, Fri 7am–8pm, Sat–Sun 9am–6pm

STUDY SPACES & ROOM RESERVATIONS
---------------------------------
Students can reserve study rooms via the campus portal at portal.ulm.edu.my
or through the Campus Assistant.

- Hartwell Hall Study Rooms (12 rooms, 2–8 person capacity)
  Reservable 1–3 hour blocks; up to 7 days in advance

- Innovation Hub Collaboration Rooms (6 rooms, 4–12 person capacity)
  Available Mon–Fri 8am–10pm, Sat 9am–6pm

- Student Centre Meeting Rooms (8 rooms, up to 20 person capacity)
  For student clubs and group projects

TRANSPORTATION
--------------
- Campus Shuttle: Free for students, runs every 15 minutes, 7am–11pm weekdays
  Routes: North Loop, South Loop, Off-Campus Residences Express

- Bike Share: 50 bikes across 8 stations on campus (RM5/hour or free with student ID)

- Parking: Lots P1–P5; permits required Mon–Fri 7am–5pm; Permit portal: parking.ulm.edu.my

- LRT/MRT: Taman Jaya station ~5-minute walk from main entrance
